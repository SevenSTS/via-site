Institute of Applied
Computer Science

In a constantly evolving sector, the Institute of Applied Computer Science enables you to adapt to the changes and complexities of the professional world.

For over 15 years, the IIA has been fostering a network of Chief Information Officers from companies in Mayenne.

These exchanges make it possible to stay closely connected to the needs and expectations of businesses. This collaboration positions the IIA at the heart of the region's digital ecosystem.

You are trained by professionals and immersed in projects where you must put your curiosity, enthusiasm, and perseverance at the service of your company. We support you in developing your interpersonal skills to facilitate your professional integration.
